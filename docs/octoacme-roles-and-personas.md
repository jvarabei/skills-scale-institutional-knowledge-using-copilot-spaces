# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises. Understanding these roles helps new team members identify their responsibilities, collaboration touchpoints, and how they contribute to successful project delivery.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile processes, remove impediments, and coach the team on agile practices. They serve the team, Product Manager, and organization to optimize delivery flow.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint ceremonies (planning, daily standup, review, retrospective)
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team members on agile principles and self-organization
- Shield the team from external interruptions during sprint execution
- Track and communicate team velocity and capacity
- Foster continuous improvement culture

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Ensure adherence to agreed agile practices
- Build a self-organizing, high-performing team
- Create psychological safety and transparency

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Weekly syncs with PM and PdM on delivery health
- Escalation of blockers and dependency issues
- Team velocity and capacity reporting

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers**: Facilitates ceremonies, removes impediments, coaches on practices
- **With PM/PdM**: Aligns on priorities, communicates team capacity and blockers
- **With Stakeholders**: Protects team focus while ensuring transparency on progress

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers research user needs, create intuitive experiences, and validate designs through testing. They ensure features are usable, accessible, and aligned with user expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define information architecture and user flows
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Ensure accessibility standards (WCAG) are met
- Validate designs with real users before and after release

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Balance user needs with business and technical constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with developers and Product Manager
- User research findings and testing reports
- Design specs and asset handoff documentation
- Feedback sessions with stakeholders and end users

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager**: Collaborates on problem definition and success metrics
- **With Developers**: Provides design specs, reviews implementations, discusses trade-offs
- **With Technical Writer**: Aligns on UI terminology and help content
- **With Support/Sales**: Gathers user feedback and pain points

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, accurate documentation for end users, developers, and internal teams. They ensure knowledge is accessible and discoverable.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API docs, and release notes
- Collaborate with developers to document features and changes
- Ensure documentation is clear, accurate, and up-to-date
- Create onboarding materials and tutorials
- Maintain documentation structure and searchability
- Review UI copy for consistency and clarity

### Goals
- Enable users to self-serve through quality documentation
- Reduce support burden with clear, accessible guides
- Ensure consistent terminology and voice across all content
- Make knowledge discoverable and maintainable

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with engineering and Product teams
- Content planning sessions aligned to release schedules
- Feedback from Support on common user issues
- Style guide updates and terminology discussions

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers**: Gathers technical details, reviews code comments and API docs
- **With UX Designer**: Aligns on terminology, creates help content for UI
- **With Support**: Incorporates feedback on user pain points into documentation
- **With Product Manager**: Understands feature context and user goals

## Stakeholder Types

Stakeholders provide input, feedback, and approvals throughout the project lifecycle. Understanding stakeholder types helps teams communicate effectively and manage expectations.

### Sales

#### Role Summary
Sales teams represent customer needs and competitive pressures. They provide market insights and need clear feature timelines to close deals.

#### Responsibilities
- Communicate customer requirements and deal blockers
- Provide competitive intelligence and market trends
- Request feature commitments for customer negotiations
- Give feedback on product positioning and messaging

#### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager**: Shares customer feedback and feature requests
- **With Project Manager**: Requests timeline clarity for deals
- **With Technical Writer**: Needs sales collateral and feature overviews

### Support

#### Role Summary
Support teams interact directly with users, identifying bugs, usability issues, and knowledge gaps. They are critical for product quality feedback.

#### Responsibilities
- Report bugs and usability issues observed in customer interactions
- Provide feedback on documentation gaps and clarity
- Communicate customer pain points and feature requests
- Validate that fixes resolve reported issues

#### Key Interactions
- **With Developers**: Reports bugs with reproduction steps
- **With UX Designer**: Shares user confusion and friction points
- **With Technical Writer**: Identifies documentation gaps and errors
- **With Product Manager**: Communicates recurring customer requests

### Compliance

#### Role Summary
Compliance stakeholders ensure projects meet regulatory, security, and legal requirements. They provide guidance and approvals for features with compliance implications.

#### Responsibilities
- Review features for regulatory and legal compliance
- Define security and privacy requirements
- Approve releases that affect compliance posture
- Provide training on compliance obligations

#### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager**: Reviews feature requirements for compliance impact
- **With Developers**: Defines security and data handling requirements
- **With Project Manager**: Communicates approval gates and timelines
- **With Technical Writer**: Reviews documentation for compliance accuracy
